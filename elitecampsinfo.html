Invite Only Elite Camp

Details

The elite camp is a 2 hour long session where Chandler Hoffman will focus on a number of areas including; finishing, ball control, off the ball movement, positioning, and technique. This session is designed for players who are aggressive in their efforts to improve their game with the intentions of moving on to the next level.

The selection process for this session is headed by Chandler Hoffman in coordination with a group of experienced local coaches. If you believe you are among the elite and would like to be considered email our offices at info@chandlerhoffman.com for evaluation.
