2013 Birmingham Winter Camps

Details

Each day's session provides the opportunity for campers to meet and train with Chandler Hoffman for 2 full hours. All sessions will start with Chandler Hoffman leading players through an official LA Galaxy warm-up followed immediately by ball work and Chandler Hoffman's personally designed finishing training.

Each session will conclude with a tournament style competition where campers will be able to showcase their talents. The player who performs the best each day will be named to the "Hoffman Players to Watch List" and will will receive a signed piece of memorabilia from Chandler Hoffman's personal collection.

All participants will have the chance to take personal photos with Chandler Hoffman and receive autographs. Every session will end with a question and answer session where campers and parents will have the opportunity to interact and converse with Chandler Hoffman.
